A Look at what's next.

The guiding intent of futureperfect is simple: to envision the future of our designed world, not merely in how it might develop, but in how it should develop. Gathering experts from the diverse fields of technology, design, and policy, we are here to discuss the convergence and connection between the physical, digital, and social.
